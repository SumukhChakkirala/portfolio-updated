  1. Load mesh and compute convex hull and offset buffer.

  3. Split hull and mesh, merge small regions for watertight halves.

  5. Repair mesh defects and export STL files.
  6. Analyze extractability and flag regions needing special treatment.

Outputs: raw + repaired metamold STLs, repair logs, and an extractability report.
